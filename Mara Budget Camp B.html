Mara Budget Camp

Welcome to Mara Budget Camp

Experience the Mara Budget Camp – a perfect combination of comfort and adventure in the heart of the Maasai Mara. With guided game drives, authentic dining, and cozy stays, it’s ideal for budget travelers who don’t want to miss the safari thrill.

Camp Amenities

Enjoy a comfortable stay with all the essentials for your safari adventure.

Cozy tents with comfortable bedding
Authentic dining with local cuisine
Complimentary Wi-Fi in common areas
24/7 security and guided escorts

Sample 3-Day Itinerary

Discover what your safari adventure at Mara Budget Camp could look like.

Day 1: Arrival & Evening Game Drive

Check into your tent, enjoy a welcome meal, and embark on an evening game drive to spot lions and elephants.

Day 2: Full-Day Safari

Explore the Mara with a full-day game drive, including a picnic lunch by the Mara River, to witness the Great Migration.

Day 3: Maasai Village & Departure

Visit a Maasai village for a cultural experience before a morning game drive and departure.
