Oilepo Amboseli

Welcome to Oilepo Amboseli

100% Maasai-owned and operated, Oilepo Amboseli is a beautiful eco-friendly luxury camp in the Kimana Sanctuary with breathtaking views of Mount Kilimanjaro. Enjoy spacious tents, authentic Maasai hospitality, cultural experiences, walking safaris, and direct support to the local community.
