Mara Chui Camp

Welcome to Mara Chui Camp

Perfectly located in the Oloolaimutia Valley, Mara Chui Camp is a family-friendly haven offering spacious tents, family suites, children’s menu, and specially trained guides who make safaris exciting and educational for kids of all ages.

Camp Amenities

Family suites, children’s menu, and child-friendly activities.

Family suites & interconnecting tents
Kids menu & high chairs
Child-friendly game drives & guides
Safe swimming pool area

Sample 3-Day Family Itinerary

Safaris designed for both adults and children to enjoy.

Day 1: Arrival & Family Game Drive

Welcome lunch, kids activities, short family-friendly evening game drive.

Day 2: Full Day Adventure

Morning game drive with kid-friendly guide, picnic lunch, afternoon activities & pool time.

Day 3: Morning Safari & Departure

Final family game drive, breakfast, Maasai cultural talk for kids, departure.
