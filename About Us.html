About Big5 Adventures

Big5 Adventures

Established in 2018, Big5 Adventures has grown from a passionate local operator into one of Kenya’s most respected and trusted safari companies. We specialize in personalized, high-quality wildlife safaris across Kenya’s iconic national parks and private conservancies — including Masai Mara, Amboseli, Tsavo, Samburu, Lake Nakuru, and the coast.

With a fleet of well-maintained 4×4 Land Cruisers and luxury vans, a team of KPSGA-certified professional guides, and partnerships with Kenya’s finest lodges and camps, we deliver seamless, unforgettable safari experiences tailored to every budget and preference.

Our Mission

To provide authentic, safe, and transformative Kenyan safari experiences that exceed expectations, while promoting responsible tourism that benefits local communities and protects Kenya’s precious wildlife and natural heritage.

Our Vision

To be East Africa’s leading safari company — recognized for excellence in service, deep respect for nature, and meaningful positive impact on the people and places we serve.
