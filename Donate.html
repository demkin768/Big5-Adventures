The Foundation

Big5 Global is committed to protecting Africa's wildlife while supporting vulnerable communities.

Across Kenya and Africa, wildlife conservation and human wellbeing are deeply connected. When communities thrive, wildlife can survive.

Our initiatives focus on wildlife conservation, anti-poaching efforts, habitat protection, and emergency humanitarian assistance for communities affected by drought, conflict, and poverty.

Your donation helps fund ranger patrols, conservation technology, community support programs, and emergency relief efforts.Together we can protect the Big Five, preserve ecosystems, and support people living alongside wildlife.

Donations made are also being chanelled towards humanitarian assistance in Sudan in partnership with The World Food Programme (WFP).The UN World Food Programme (WFP) is a critical lifeline for millions of people, with emergency food assistance helping to reverse famine conditions in the region.

By contributing to this campaign, you become part of a global movement working to safeguard Africa's wildlife and help communities build a safer and more sustainable future.
