Severin Safari Camp

Welcome to Severin Safari Camp

An award-winning luxury tented camp in the heart of Tsavo West, Severin Safari Camp combines elegant canvas tents with wooden floors, a renowned gourmet restaurant, wellness spa, swimming pool, and exceptional personal service. Perfect for discerning travelers seeking comfort without sacrificing the authentic bush experience.
