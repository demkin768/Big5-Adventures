The Foundation

Big5 Global is committed to protecting Africa's wildlife while supporting vulnerable communities.

Across Kenya and Africa, wildlife conservation and human wellbeing are deeply connected. When communities thrive, wildlife can survive.

Our initiatives focus on wildlife conservation, anti-poaching efforts, habitat protection, and emergency humanitarian assistance for communities affected by drought, conflict, and poverty. We set the precedent by channeling 30% of profits from Big5 Adventures towards wildlife conservation and community projects.

Your donation helps fund ranger patrols, conservation technology, community support programs, and emergency relief efforts. Together we can protect the Big Five, preserve natural ecosystems, and support people who depend on these landscapes for their livelihoods.

Donations made in support of Sudan are channeled towards humanitarian assistance in partnership with The World Food Programme (WFP).Almost 19.5 million people are facing acute hunger due to the devastating civil conflict.The UN World Food Programme (WFP) is a critical lifeline for millions of people, with emergency food assistance helping to reverse famine conditions in the region. This is in support of the 17 UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

By contributing to this campaign, you become part of a global movement working to safeguard Africa's wildlife and help communities build a safer and more sustainable future.
