Discover Diani Beach

Diani Beach, located 600 km from Nairobi along Kenya’s south coast, is a tropical paradise with white sandy beaches, turquoise waters, and vibrant coral reefs. Renowned for its water sports and relaxed vibe, it’s perfect for adventure and relaxation.

From snorkeling in Kisite-Mpunguti Marine Park to dining on fresh seafood, Diani offers a coastal escape like no other.

Frequently Asked Questions

What’s the best time to visit Diani Beach?

April to October offers warm, dry weather ideal for beach activities and water sports. November to March is hotter but still enjoyable.

What water activities are available in Diani?

Diani offers snorkeling, scuba diving, kite surfing, deep-sea fishing, and dolphin-watching boat tours.

What should I pack for Diani Beach?

Pack swimwear, sunscreen, sunglasses, light clothing, a hat, water shoes, and a light cover-up for evenings.

Is Diani Beach safe for swimming?

Yes, Diani’s beaches are generally safe for swimming, with protected coral reefs. Always follow local guides’ advice.

Can I combine Diani with other destinations?

Yes, Diani pairs well with Tsavo for a safari-beach combo or nearby Watamu for more coastal exploration.

How long should I stay in Diani Beach?

A 3-5 day stay allows time for beach relaxation, water sports, and cultural experiences.
