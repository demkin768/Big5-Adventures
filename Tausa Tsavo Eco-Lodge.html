Tausa Tsavo Eco-Lodge

Welcome to Tausa Tsavo Eco-Lodge

A true eco-retreat in the heart of Tsavo West, Tausa Eco-Lodge combines sustainable luxury with intimate wildlife encounters. Fully solar-powered, built with local materials, serving organic farm-to-table meals, and offering guided nature walks and game drives with minimal environmental impact.

Sample 3-Day Eco-Safari

Low-impact, high-wonder experience.

Day 1: Arrival & Sunset Walk

Transfer in, eco-tour of lodge, guided sunset nature walk.

Day 2: Full Day Immersion

Morning game drive, visit organic farm, afternoon walking safari, stargazing.

Bird walk or short game drive, breakfast, departure.
