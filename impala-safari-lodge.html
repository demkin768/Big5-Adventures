Impala Safari Lodge

Welcome to Impala Safari Lodge

A long-established favourite in Tsavo West, Impala Safari Lodge offers spacious, air-conditioned rooms, a large swimming pool, generous buffet dining, and a relaxed atmosphere. Centrally located for easy access to Mzima Springs, Shetani Lava Flow, and the Chyulu Hills.

Lodge Amenities

Comfort and convenience in the heart of Tsavo West.

Spacious air-conditioned rooms
Large swimming pool
Generous buffet dining
Daily game drives included

Sample 3-Day Itinerary

Perfect base for exploring Tsavo West’s highlights.

Day 1: Arrival & Evening Drive

Transfer in, lunch, relax by the pool, evening game drive.

Day 2: Full Day Safari

Visit Mzima Springs, Shetani Lava Flow, afternoon game drive.

Day 3: Morning Drive & Departure

Final morning game drive, breakfast, departure.
