Elewana Tortilis Camp Amboseli

Welcome to Elewana Tortilis Camp Amboseli

Named after the iconic Tortilis acacia trees, this multi-award-winning camp is set in a private 30,000-acre conservancy with unrivalled views of Mount Kilimanjaro. Offering elegant makuti-thatched tents with private verandas, a stunning swimming pool, exceptional cuisine, spa, and exclusive activities including walking safaris and private game drives.

Leading Conservation Through Tourism

A pioneer in community-owned conservancies.

Elewana Tortilis Camp operates in the Kitirua Conservancy – one of Kenya’s most successful community conservation models. Lease fees go directly to Maasai landowners, supporting education, healthcare, and wildlife protection.
