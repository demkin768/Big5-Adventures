AA Lodge Mara

Welcome to AA Lodge Mara

AA Lodge Mara is a luxurious safari lodge offering elegant rooms with private verandas, a sparkling swimming pool, spa, restaurant, and unbeatable location just minutes from Talek Gate. Enjoy comfort, style, and exceptional wildlife viewing.

Lodge Amenities

Luxury accommodation with all modern comforts in the heart of the Mara.

Elegant rooms with private veranda
Large swimming pool & sundeck
Spa & wellness center
Fine dining restaurant & bar

Sample 3-Day Itinerary

Experience luxury and adventure with daily game drives and relaxation time.

Day 1: Arrival & Evening Game Drive

Transfer from Nairobi, welcome drink, lunch, relax by the pool, evening game drive.

Day 2: Full Day in the Mara

Early morning game drive, breakfast, relax or spa, afternoon game drive with sundowner.

Day 3: Morning Game Drive & Departure

Final morning game drive, full breakfast, check-out and transfer back to Nairobi.
