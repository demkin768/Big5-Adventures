Amboseli Serena Lodge

Welcome to Amboseli Serena Lodge

Set against the magnificent backdrop of Mount Kilimanjaro, Africa’s highest mountain, Amboseli Serena Safari Lodge offers elegant Maasai-inspired rooms, fine dining with wine pairing, a large swimming pool, and one of the best locations inside Amboseli National Park for viewing large elephant herds.
