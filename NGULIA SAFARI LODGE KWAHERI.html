Ngulia Safari Lodge

Welcome to Ngulia Safari Lodge

Perched dramatically on the edge of the Ndawe Escarpment with panoramic views over the Rhino Valley, Ngulia Safari Lodge is famous for its floodlit watering hole that attracts leopards almost nightly. Offering comfortable rooms, a large swimming pool, excellent buffet dining, and one of the most spectacular locations in all of Tsavo West.
